Fried Rice

Description

In Nigerian cuisine, Fried Rice holds a special place as a festive and celebratory dish, often served at weddings, parties, and holidays. It's a colorful and flavorful dish where cooked rice is stir-fried with a vibrant mix of vegetables such as carrots, green peas, and bell peppers. These vegetables not only add a variety of textures but also enhance the visual appeal of the dish, making it a centerpiece on any Nigerian dining table.

What sets Nigerian Fried Rice apart is its unique seasoning and additions. Alongside the vegetables, it's common to find diced chicken, shrimp, or liver, adding protein-rich elements that make the dish more substantial. Onions, garlic, and sometimes ginger are used generously to infuse the rice with a fragrant and savory flavor profile. Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably leftover and cold)
  • Assorted vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • Cooked shrimp, chicken, or ham (optional)
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Green onions (scallions)

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Gather cooked rice (preferably leftover and cold), assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers), cooked shrimp, chicken or ham (optional), eggs, soy sauce, vegetable oil, garlic, onions, and green onions.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and garlic. Saut√© until onions are translucent and garlic is fragrant.
  4. If using eggs, push the onions and garlic to the side of the skillet. Crack eggs into the skillet and scramble until fully cooked. Break up the eggs into small pieces with a spatula.
  5. Add cooked shrimp, chicken or ham (if using), and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  6. Add assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers) and stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. Add cold, cooked rice to the skillet. Break up any clumps and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, tossing constantly to heat the rice evenly.
  8. Drizzle soy sauce over the rice and toss well to combine. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  9. Continue to stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the fried rice is heated through and evenly coated with soy sauce.
  10. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.
  11. Serve hot as a main dish or side dish.
